About

Welcome to La-Unique

La Unique, directly means "You Unique", and along with our slogan "be treated like it", you get the impression that "You are Unique" and should "be treated like it"... And this is completely true. Here at La-Unique Health and Beauty Salon we do believe that everyone is Unique and that everyone should be treated like it. We live by this motto. We are this motto.
La-Unique ... Be Treated Like It.

A Journey Through Time

- The History -

The History

La-Unique Health and Beauty first open its doors in Middelburg Mpumalanga in 2015. As mentioned previously the idea behind La-Unique was, "you are unique, and you should be treated like it". Back in Middelburg, we were a prestigious, well known, and trusted salon among many, but unfortunately we had to close our doors in 2021 and relocate. But we are proud to say that we reopened in July of 2022 in the beautiful town on Hartbeesport dam in the North West Province where we now call home. We are still the well established salon we were in Middelburg, the only difference is where we are located...

The Perfect Place

- The Salon -

The Salon

As of 2022 La-Unique salon is based at Homestead, Broderstroom, Hartbeesport, on the R512. The salon is in an 1900's farm house converted to a "Trading House". Within Homestead theres a carwash, restaurant, and deli among other things coming. The salon has its own dedicated room with bathroom ...Its cozy, clean, and professional. Come through, wash your car, pamper yourself, and have a burger or waffle while you are at it. Convenient right?
