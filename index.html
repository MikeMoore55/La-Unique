The History

La-Unique Health and Beauty first open its doors in Middleburg Mpumalanga in 2015. As mentioned previously the idea behind La-Unique was, you are unique, and you should be treated like it. Back in Middleburg, we were a prestigious, well known, and trusted salon among many, but unfortunately we had to close our doors in 2021 and relocate. But we are proud to say that we reopened in July of 2022 in the beautiful town on Hartbeesport dam in the North West Province where we now call home. We are still the well established salon we were in Middleburg, the only difference is where we are located...

The Salon

As of 2022 the salon is based at Homestead, Broderstroom, Hartbeesport, on the R512. The salon is in an 1900's farm house converted to a "Trading House". Within Homestead theres a carwash, restaurant, and deli among other things coming. The salon has its own dedicated room with bathroom ...Its cozy, clean, and professional. Come through, wash your car, pamper yourself, and have a burger or waffle while you are at it. Convenient right?
